How do I restore my subscription?

Open Settings → Restore purchases. Your Premium access will be restored on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Manage everything in your Apple ID: Settings app → your name → Subscriptions, or from within SourceMint under Settings → Manage subscription.

I have a promo code

Open Settings → Redeem Code (or “Redeem a code” on the paywall) and enter it. Codes are single-use and unlock Premium for their stated duration.

Are the margins accurate?

Margin figures are transparent estimates based on standard Amazon referral and FBA fee models plus your landed cost. Fees and prices vary and change over time — always verify before ordering. Supplier and product data in this version is curated sample data.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Browsing cached data, saved suppliers and your Premium status all work without a connection.
